Privacy Policy

Effective date: 27/06/2024

This Privacy Policy explains how GetMark ("Extension", "we", "our") handles your information while using the extension on Google Chrome.

What Data We Collect

1. Notes and Tags (User Input)

All notes, tags, and URLs that you manually add while using GetMark are stored locally in Chrome’s synchronized storage (using chrome.storage.sync).

This allows your notes to be available across your Chrome profiles where you are logged in.

2. URLs of Active Tabs

GetMark reads the URL of the active tab when you create a note. This is solely used to associate your note with the corresponding webpage. We do not monitor your browsing or store URLs automatically.

3. No Analytics, No Tracking

GetMark does not collect any analytics, usage statistics, personal identifiers, or browsing history. We do not use any third-party analytics services or ad networks.

4. No Server Storage

We do not operate any server or cloud infrastructure. All your data remains local to your Chrome account via Chrome Sync storage.

Permissions Explained

activeTab

Allows the extension to access the current page URL only when you manually create a note.

storage

Enables saving and syncing your notes, tags, and preferences locally via Chrome’s chrome.storage API.

host_permissions

Allows associating notes with specific domains/pages for better search and filtering functionality.

No Remote Code Execution

GetMark does not load or execute any code from external sources. All code runs entirely within the extension package reviewed by Chrome Web Store.

How We Use Your Information

  • To provide the note-taking functionality tied to specific webpages
  • To allow searching, sorting, and filtering your personal notes
  • To enable syncing your data across Chrome devices

How We Do Not Use Your Information

  • We do not share or sell your data to third parties
  • We do not collect your browsing history
  • We do not run ads or targeted marketing
  • We do not store any of your data on external servers

Data Security

Since all your data remains inside your Chrome storage, its security depends on the security of your Google Account and Chrome browser.

We do not access your data unless you voluntarily provide information during support conversations.

Your Rights & Data Deletion

You may delete your notes at any time directly through the extension interface. If you uninstall GetMark, all data stored in Chrome’s synchronized storage will be removed automatically.

If you have any questions or wish to request manual data deletion assistance, contact us at waskekrishna11@gmail.com.
