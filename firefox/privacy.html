Privacy Policy (Firefox Edition)

Effective Date: 27/06/2024

This Privacy Policy explains how the GetMark Firefox Extension ("Extension", "we", "our") handles your data while using the extension through Mozilla Firefox.

Data We Collect & Store

1. User Notes & Tags

All notes and tags you manually create are stored locally in your Firefox profile using browser.storage.sync. This enables syncing your notes across your Firefox devices where you're signed into your Firefox account.

2. Active Tab URL (Only When Used)

The extension reads the URL of the active tab only when you create a note. This is used to link your note to the page or domain you’re referencing. We do not log or track browsing history.

3. No Automatic Data Collection

We do not collect any automatic telemetry, analytics, or personal information. We do not track your activity, behavior, or browser usage in any way.

4. No Remote Servers

All data is stored locally on your Firefox profile and synchronized using Firefox Sync (if enabled). We do not operate any external server or database. No data is sent or uploaded to any third party.

Permissions Explanation

activeTab

Required to access the current tab’s URL for associating notes with the page you are viewing.

storage

Used to store your notes, tags, and settings inside the Firefox browser securely.

host_permissions

Allows associating notes to specific domains for filtering and search purposes. No content from pages is accessed or read.

No Remote Code Execution

The extension does not load or execute any code from external sources. All logic is bundled within the signed WebExtension package.

How We Use Your Information

  • To allow you to create, view, edit, and organize notes tied to specific webpages.
  • To provide search, filtering, and sorting capabilities across your notes.
  • To optionally sync your notes across Firefox devices if Firefox Sync is enabled by the user.

How We Do Not Use Your Information

  • We do not share, sell, or monetize your data.
  • We do not collect your browsing history or search activity.
  • We do not serve advertisements or marketing based on your data.
  • We do not store any of your data on any remote server controlled by us or third parties.

Security of Your Data

Your data remains secured within your Firefox profile and/or Firefox Sync. We do not have access to your data. You control the retention and deletion of your own notes and tags.

User Controls

You can delete any note, tag, or all your data at any time through the extension interface. Uninstalling the extension removes all associated data from your browser.

Data Disclosure

Since we do not store your data, we have no data to disclose to third parties, law enforcement, or any government agency.

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy when releasing new features or updates required by Mozilla AMO policies. You will always be able to review the latest version on our official website or store listing.
